隐私声明

本声明说明 MCEO DISC 成交沟通测评™（下称「本系统」）如何收集、使用与保护你的个人资料。本系统面向马来西亚用户，依据《2010 年个人资料保护法令》(PDPA) 的一般原则处理资料。

我们收集什么

姓名、Email、WhatsApp 号码、公司与行业（选填）、身份，以及你的测评作答与结果。为安全统计，我们会保存你 IP 地址的不可逆哈希值（不保存完整 IP）与浏览器的简化资料（设备类型／浏览器名称）。

我们如何使用

用于生成你的测评报告、与你就报告及相关服务进行后续联系，以及匿名的整体统计分析。未经你同意，我们不会将你的资料用于与上述无关的营销。

资料分享

我们不会出售你的个人资料。除非法律要求，我们不会将可识别你的资料提供给无关的第三方。资料储存于 Supabase（云端数据库服务）。

Cookie 与本地储存

为记住你的语言偏好与作答进度，本系统会在你的浏览器使用 localStorage（本地储存）。这些资料只存在你的设备上，你可随时清除浏览器资料将其删除。

你的权利

你有权查阅、更正或要求删除你的个人资料。如需删除，请来信 callme@mcjo.com.my，并提供你测评时使用的姓名与 Email。

免责

本测评为商业培训与自我觉察工具，非临床心理测试、医疗诊断或职业预测。

Privacy Notice

This notice explains how the MCEO DISC Sales & Communication Profile™ ("the System") collects, uses and protects your personal data. The System serves users in Malaysia and follows the general principles of the Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA).

What we collect

Your name, email, WhatsApp number, company and industry (optional), your role, and your assessment answers and results. For secure analytics we store an irreversible hash of your IP address (never the full IP) and a simplified browser summary (device type / browser name).

How we use it

To generate your report, to follow up about the report and related services, and for anonymous aggregate analytics. We will not use your data for unrelated marketing without your consent.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We will not share identifiable data with unrelated third parties except where required by law. Data is stored with Supabase (a cloud database service).

Cookies & local storage

To remember your language preference and answering progress, the System uses your browser's localStorage. This stays on your device only, and you can remove it by clearing your browser data.

Your rights

You may access, correct or request deletion of your personal data. For deletion, email callme@mcjo.com.my with the name and email you used.

Disclaimer

This assessment is a business-training and self-awareness tool — not a clinical test, medical diagnosis or career prediction.
